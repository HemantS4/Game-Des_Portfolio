What does water do?

We began by mapping where water lives.

Properties We Noticed

  • Fluid vs. Solid vs. Vapour → water slips between states.
  • Vital yet Volatile → life-giver and destroyer.

This duality fascinated us.

Conclusion & Reflection

Flow & Hold emerged from a simple question about water's dual nature and evolved into a philosophical card game that challenges players to think beyond traditional binaries. Through iterative design, playtesting, and thoughtful visual development, we created a game that mirrors water's fundamental properties—containment and continuum, held and flowing.

The game invites players to question their relationship with water and the containers we place around it—both literal and metaphorical. By engaging with these concepts through strategic gameplay, players develop a deeper appreciation for water's complexity and the arbitrary boundaries we impose on nature.

Strike the balance. Water doesn't choose between flow and hold—it exists in both states simultaneously. The game asks: can you?
