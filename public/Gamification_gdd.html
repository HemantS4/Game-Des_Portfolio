1. High Concept

Momentum is a gamified documentation and journaling app that helps students, designers, and professionals capture, organize, and grow their ideas while staying motivated through game mechanics such as XP, streaks, badges, and unlockable themes. It combines AI assistance with habit-forming design to make documentation both effortless and rewarding.

  2. Organize: AI formats and categorizes the entry.
  3. Earn: Gain XP and streak progress.

14. Key Takeaways

Four critical insights emerged from the design and research process:

TakeawayInsight
1. Reducing Friction = RetentionSimple capture methods (voice, photo, quick text) were the #1 driver for daily use. Every extra tap or screen reduces engagement.
2. Gamification Builds HabitsStreaks and challenges proved crucial for encouraging consistency. Users responded to visible progress markers that celebrated effort, not perfection.
3. AI Adds Value, Not ComplexityUsers loved AI summaries and insights that revealed patterns in their entries. However, they rejected AI-written entries, preferring authentic self-expression with AI as a supportive tool.
4. Personalization = ConnectionAllowing users to customize themes, avatars, and visual styles made the documentation space feel "theirs," increasing emotional investment and long-term retention.

These learnings shaped a product that respects user agency while providing structure, support, and celebration.
